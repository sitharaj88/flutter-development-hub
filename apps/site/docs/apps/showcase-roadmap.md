# Showcase Roadmap

The apps area can become one of the most persuasive parts of the repository when it moves from placeholder ideas to visible examples.

## High-value app categories

- clean showcase apps that demonstrate UI quality and architecture judgment
- cohort lab apps that support training delivery
- starter kits that help teams begin with better defaults
- internal tools that show operational thinking beyond the classroom

## What these apps should prove

- ability to turn concepts into maintainable execution
- fluency in Flutter beyond presentation-level knowledge
- strong engineering habits around structure, delivery, and reuse

## Good sequencing

- begin with one polished showcase app
- add one training lab starter project
- expand into reusable kits or internal workflow tools
