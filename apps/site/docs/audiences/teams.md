# For Teams

Teams care about delivery outcomes, consistency, and whether the training improves how engineering actually works.

## What matters most here

- adoption without delivery chaos
- engineering standards that hold up under product and stakeholder pressure
- training that connects directly to the current roadmap, codebase, or operating model

## Team-ready messaging

- align the curriculum with live product priorities
- combine capability building with implementation guidance
- reduce risk while making Flutter delivery more predictable and more mature
