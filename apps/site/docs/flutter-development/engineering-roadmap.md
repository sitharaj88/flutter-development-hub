# Engineering Roadmap

Because this repository is meant to hold both the docs site and future apps, it helps to think ahead about what can live here later.

## Short term

- complete the public-facing training and Flutter services pages
- add brochure download support
- add enquiry forms, testimonials, and proof points

## Medium term

- publish demo Flutter apps and reference implementations in `apps/`
- document training labs and starter projects
- add case studies, workshops, and event landing pages

## Long term

- host reusable course material and guided learning paths
- maintain client-ready architecture notes and engineering standards
- turn the site into the main knowledge and delivery hub for your Flutter practice
