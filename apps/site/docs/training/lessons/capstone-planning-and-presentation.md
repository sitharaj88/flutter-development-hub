# Capstone Planning and Presentation

The final lesson should help learners turn their skill into visible proof.

## Lesson goal

- plan a small but complete application
- make thoughtful decisions about design and structure
- present the result in a way that shows understanding

## What to teach

- choosing a scope that is realistic
- defining user flow and feature priorities
- documenting key decisions and tradeoffs
- presenting the project clearly to reviewers, teams, or interviewers

## Good capstone features

- authentication or onboarding
- dashboard or list-detail flow
- API-backed content
- form interaction and validation
- clean structure and reusable UI

## Teaching outcome

- learners finish the training with something they can explain, improve, and showcase
