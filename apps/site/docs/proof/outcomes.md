# Outcomes

This page is where the trust layer becomes concrete. Even before you publish hard numbers or client logos, it should explain what kinds of outcomes the engagement is designed to create.

## Outcome categories

- stronger team confidence in Flutter delivery
- clearer engineering practices and more reusable implementation habits
- faster onboarding for new developers entering the stack
- more credible learning outcomes for students and institutions
- better delivery stability for product teams

## Useful proof points to collect over time

- projects delivered or stabilized
- workshops or batches completed
- engineering or learning milestones achieved
- testimonials from leaders, teams, or participants
- evidence of stronger standards, documentation, or implementation quality

## Good evidence formats

- short written testimonials
- before-and-after delivery stories
- screenshots, diagrams, and architecture snapshots
- concrete indicators where available without exaggeration
